Cardio Burn

Ignite Metabolism & Build Endurance

About the Program

Our Cardio Burn program is designed to test your limits, torch body fat, and optimize cardiovascular conditioning. Featuring high-intensity interval training (HIIT), dynamic circuits, and endurance challenges, this class is structured to stimulate long-term fat loss while fortifying your heart health. Under the guidance of our specialist coaches, you will engage in targeted sessions utilizing the Treadmill, stationary Cycling protocols, and athletic drills. Whether your goal is to boost your VO2 max or achieve a lean physique, this program offers scientific programming to get you there.

Program Benefits

  • Substantial improvement in stamina, endurance, and heart health
  • High calorie burn both during and hours after the workout via HIIT
  • Varied conditioning workouts including Running, cycling, and rowing
  • Highly optimized programming targeted for sustainable fat loss

Who is it for?

Perfect for individuals looking to shed excess body weight, improve cardiovascular capabilities, and enjoy high-energy group workouts that keep you moving.
